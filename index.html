Education Levels in Relation to Family Size

What is Education?

Education is a child's way to a better future. Every child should be able to learn and grow. But in the Philippines, many families struggle because school is expensive and they have many children. Parents often have to decide whether to send all their kids to school or just the ones they can afford. This website explores how family size affects education, and why every child deserves a fair chance to learn, dream, and succeed.
